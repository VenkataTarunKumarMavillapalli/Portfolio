02 : Projects

I am a final-year Data Science Masters student with a passion for leveraging data to derive meaningful insights. My projects include Fake and Real News Detection using a Chatbot, where I implemented data cleaning, EDA, TF-IDF, 15 machine learning algorithms and finally a BERT-based chatbot for news authenticity prediction. In Forecasting Weight Dynamics, I applied time series analysis and developed a Power BI dashboard for insect colony weight visualization. For Human Activity Recognition, I conducted statistical data analysis, using various classification models and K-fold cross-validation. Missing Value Imputation for the Titanic Dataset involved OpenMP and cluster computing. Predicting Housing Prices in California Census Data was accomplished by exploring, pre-processing, and fine-tuning a Random Forest model. Google Business Intelligence and Data Analytics Capstone projects showcased my practical skills in business intelligence and data analysis, using Google DataFlow, BigQuery, and Python, data cleaning, transformation, visualization, and analysis to create a BI viz addressing project goals, also explored trends, identified opportunities for growth, and suggested marketing strategies. The IBM Machine Learning Capstone involved implementing a recommender system utilized various unsupervised and supervised learning algorithms for content-based and collaborative-filtering recommendations. Finally, the Google Project Management Capstone, Sauce & Spoon, demonstrated my project management expertise, from analyzing requirements to effective communication and problem-solving. Overall, my diverse project portfolio reflects my proficiency in data science, machine learning, and project management, ready to tackle real-world challenges in these domains.
