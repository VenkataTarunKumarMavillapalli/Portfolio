002 : Quality Management Systems

Issuing Organization : British Standards Institution(BSI)

Certificate No: AUBSI-QMS01-1823-0012

BSI is a British Standards Institution that provides certification services for a variety of standards, including quality management systems. BSI is one of the world's leading providers of quality management system certification services. The BSI Quality Management Systems standard is based on the international standard ISO 9001:2015. ISO 9001 is the most widely recognized quality management system standard in the world.

The BSI Quality Management Systems standard provides a framework for organizations to improve their quality management practices and ensure that they consistently meet customer requirements.

The standard covers the following topics:

1.Customer focus

2.Leadership

3.Planning

4.Support

5.Operation

6.Performance

7.Evaluation

8.Improvement

The BSI Quality Management Systems standard is designed to help organizations improve their quality management practices and reduce the likelihood of defects and errors. The standard can be used by organizations of all sizes and in all industries.
